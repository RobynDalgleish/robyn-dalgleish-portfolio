About

Hello! My name is Robyn Dalgleish, and I am a front-end web developer based in Toronto. I am an enthusiastic and positive person, who thrives in challenging situations. I absolutely love coding. To me, it feels like writing poetry; a pursuit to find the most elegant and clean way to convey an experience. I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Guelph, with a focus on Studio Art, and am a recent graduate of HackerYou College of Technology. For as long as I can remember, I have been passionate about the intersection of Art, Technology, and making space for positive change. When I'm not coding, you'll find me jumping around at the MonkeyVault parkour gym, or diving into any extreme sport I can get my hands on. Want to connect? Send me a message, and let's grab a coffee!
